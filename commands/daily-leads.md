Pull my most recent Starbridge buying signals across the buyers I follow and turn them into a prioritized "what to work today" list.

$ARGUMENTS

Steps:
1. Get the cross-buyer signal feed (the subscribed-feed scope — no single buyer).
2. Group the signals by recency, buyer, and type.
3. Rank them into a short action list. For each item give: the buyer, a one-line "why it matters," and a concrete next action (who to reach out to / what to look at).
4. If I gave a focus above, filter to it; otherwise cover the whole feed.

Keep it skimmable. Don't pad with signals that have no clear action.
